Impact

Social media’s impact on young adults and teenagers is positive because social media platforms create spaces for similar people to communicate ideas without being restricted by regional means.

Also, the ideas posted on social platforms that raise awareness for certain groups can impact the representation of minority groups positively by bringing those groups to light and/or including them.

This creates a more united and accepting world.

(S., Noor Al-Deen & Hendricks, 2012)

Conclusion

Although social media is regarded as a brainless and brain-melting waste-of-time, it is actually beneficial to the social development of teenagers and young adults and can even make them more critical and concise writers through the sharing and connection of their ideas on the social platforms.
